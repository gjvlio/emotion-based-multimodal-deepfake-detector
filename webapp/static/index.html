Emotion gap per feeling (Δ)

Δ (delta) measures, for each emotion, how much the voice and the face disagree — from 0 (perfect agreement) to 1 (total disagreement). Authentic clips stay low across the board; a big spike on any emotion is a classic sign of a stitched-together fake.

// THE THESIS

A multimodal, emotion-aware approach to deepfake detection

InstitutionPolytechnic University of the Philippines — Manila
ProgramBachelor of Science in Computer Science
Academic Year2025–2026

The full paper is confidential and available on request.

Advances in artificial intelligence have made deepfakes, synthetic media that manipulate a person's face, voice, or both that are increasingly realistic. Existing detectors perform well in controlled settings but depend on the visual artifacts left by generative models, leaving them prone to obsolescence as those models improve. This study proposes a multimodal detection framework that instead uses cross-modal emotional inconsistency as its primary signal. Genuine human emotion is biologically coordinated across facial expression, vocal tone, and linguistic content: a coherence that current deepfake pipelines cannot reproduce, since each modality is synthesized independently. Three encoders extract features: Vision Transformer (facial expression), Wav2Vec 2.0 (vocal prosody), and BERT (linguistic sentiment), that are fused through bilinear pooling to capture pairwise interactions between modalities. Trained on CREMA-D, MELD, and CMU-MOSEI with synthetic deepfake augmentation, and benchmarked on DFDC, WildDeepfake, FakeAVCeleb, and DigiFakeAV, the model is evaluated by accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, and AUC. The study assesses how reliably audio–visual emotional mismatch separates genuine from deepfake content across generation methods and real-world recording conditions.

DeepSentinel is an undergraduate thesis project built across data generation, multimodal feature engineering, model training, and evaluation. Each member led a pillar of the system while contributing to the shared goal: a deepfake detector that reasons about emotional coherence between voice and face.
